Capabilities

Agents that actually understand
your Shopify business

Each agent specializes in a domain of your store operations, trained on Shopify-specific workflows and your historical data.

Customer Support Agent

Handles returns, shipping inquiries, product questions, and order status requests using your store policies. Escalates edge cases to your team with full context. Speaks your brand voice, not canned responses.

Inventory Agent

Predicts stockouts before they happen, auto-generates purchase orders, and syncs across warehouses and channels.

Marketing Agent

Creates and sends abandoned cart sequences, post-purchase flows, and promotional campaigns matched to customer segments.

Analytics Agent

Generates weekly P&L snapshots, identifies top and underperforming SKUs, and flags anomalies in traffic or conversion.

Always On, Always Learning

Agents operate around the clock across time zones. They learn from every interaction, adapting to seasonal trends, new products, and shifting customer behavior. No retraining needed—they evolve with your store.
