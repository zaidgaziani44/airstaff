Capabilities

6 AI agents. One Shopify store.
Zero extra staff.

Every agent is trained on Shopify-specific workflows and your store's data — so they work exactly like a team member would.

💬

AI Customer Support

Handles returns, shipping inquiries, order status, and product questions — using your exact store policies. Speaks your brand voice. Escalates only what needs a human. Works 24/7 without breaks.

📦

AI Inventory Manager

Predicts stockouts before they happen. Auto-generates purchase orders. Sends alerts when stock is low. Syncs across all your channels.

✉️

AI Gmail Auto-Reply

Reads, categorizes, and replies to customer emails automatically. Handles FAQs, order updates, and complaints — all in your tone.

🔄

AI Returns Handler

Automates your entire return process. Approves eligible returns, sends labels, updates inventory, and keeps customers informed — zero manual work.

📢

AI Marketing Agent

Creates abandoned cart sequences, post-purchase flows, and promo campaigns matched to customer behavior. Adjusts messaging automatically based on performance.

📊

Profit & Loss Dashboard

Real-time P&L tracking. Weekly revenue reports. Identifies top and underperforming products. Know exactly where your money is going — always.
